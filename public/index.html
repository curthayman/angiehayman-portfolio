About Me

Hi, I’m Angie — a creative soul with a lot of tabs open (in my brain and on my browser).

By day, I help manage our family-run business, HayTree Web Services, where I handle social media and assist with design ideas while my husband Curt does the tech wizardry and builds the actual websites. We also co-own Iron Fence Cyber Solutions, focused on keeping small businesses safe online.

Outside the screen, I stay busy with things that make me happy:

  • 🪴 tending to my plants,
  • 🧶 making macrame pieces,
  • 🍳 cooking up calorie-hacked comfort food,
  • 🧵 learning to sew from scratch,
  • 🐾 and managing the surprisingly glamorous social media life of our dog, Marley.

Family is at the heart of everything I do — we’ve got a big, beautiful, Brady Bunch-style crew and a house full of laughs, love, and laundry.

For over a year now, I’ve also been volunteering with the High Point Creative Committee, designing event flyers to help bring joy and connection to our community.

This site is my little corner of the internet — a space to share what I’m working on, what I’m learning, and what brings me joy.

Thanks for stopping by. 💛

Remember to water your plants!
